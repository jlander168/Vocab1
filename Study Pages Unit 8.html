Used to add borders to any part of a table

Borders from adjacent elements are merged together to form a single border
Spanning Cells
Single cells that occupies more than one cell row or column
Row Groups

Flexboxes

Visual Viewport
Visual portion of a screen excluding on-screen keyboards, areas outside of a pinch-zoom area, or any other on-screen artifact that doesn't scale with the dimensions of a page.
